# Chapter 2 Postpaid Payment

# 2.1 Basic description

Post-paid call charge collection refers to the process of collecting customer fees by inquiring the customer's history and current account information, and simultaneously triggering the cancellation request. The specific processing is done through the front desk interaction between the sales staff and the customer or the customer's self-help.

# 2.2 Business Process

# 2.2.1 Main Process

  1. Click on the Payment menu.
  2. Enter mobile phone number / user code / account number on the page;
  3. After clicking the query, the account fee information will be displayed;
  4. Click on the ledger details to see the user's account and the corresponding balance;
  5. Amount corresponding box, enter the recharge amount, the unit is yuan;
  6. Select the ledger type and recharge to the corresponding ledger.
  7. Click the advanced option to choose cash or check payment, and fill in the payment remarks.
  8. Click the "SUbmit" button or click the corresponding amount on the screen to submit a recharge request to the background.
  9. The background receives the query request, as follows steps 10-17:
  10. If you pay by check, you need to deduct the balance of the check
  11. Pay the recharge amount to the corresponding account book; (AM_BALANCE_MM, AM_BAL_IN, AM_BAL_OUT)
  12. Record payment and statistics log. (AM_BUSI, i_Busi_Bal_Staff)
  13. Judgment of agent risk control
  14. Make monthly account write-offs to the account and support synchronous / asynchronous processing of write-offs. (AM_Writeoff, am_bill_item)
  15. Generate asset issue work order to upload account assets to ABM, trigger shutdown and start, asset upload is incremental. (Ca_Asset_Syn)
  16. Post-paid payment without texting;
  17. Pop-up recharge success prompt;
  18. print the receipt;

# 2.2.2 Business Rules

  1. Payment rules

    a. The payment amount can only be> = 0.

    b. Accounts must be specified.

    c. Only pay for post-paid users

    d. Check payment needs to call the check management interface to verify the check limit

    e. Credit card payment is made by offline credit card, no account management system is required to connect with the credit card device, just enter the relevant information

    f. Insufficient staff quota to pay fees

  2. Write-off rules

    a. Read past monthly bills for your account

    b. Read the account data of the account

    c. Ledger ordering, the rules are as follows

    Special funds priority> Low priority of ledger> Low priority of invalidation time> Non-refundable priority is higher than refundable> Passbook priority is higher than cash passbook> Priority of small effective time> Priority of small book ID

    d. Ordering of bills, the rules are as follows

    Small priority on checkout day> Priority on negative bills> Small priority on accounts> Small priority on user ID> Small priority on account ID

    e. Use the sorted ledger to cycle the reversed sorted bill

    Ordinary accounts can be written off normally according to the sorted bills and books and cancellation rules;

    A negative bill account exists, and the negative bill is treated as an ordinary cash passbook (the account deposited by the user at the front desk through cash recharge), with the same priority as the cash passbook

    f. Record write-off results

    g. Abnormal recording error log

    If the account cannot be cancelled for some reason, an error log is recorded

# Chapter 3 Account Fund Transfer

# 3.1 Basic description

By entering the transfer-in and transfer-out numbers / accounts, the account funds are transferred.

Support postpaid to postpaid / prepaid to prepaid

# 3.2 Business Process

  1. Click the Account Transfer menu to enter the page;

  2. Click Select Transfer Out Number.

  3. Select to search by Telephone Number or Account Id

  4. The background calls the three-family information interface to get the three-family information according to the mobile phone number or account id

  5. Determine the user's payment type according to the three households' data. If it is prepaid, call the prepaid balance query interface. If it is postpaid, call the postpaid balance query interface. (The prepaid balance is composed of dbm and published data. It currently returns. Should only post data on assets ca_asset_syn)

  6. After the query, the asset account and balance will be displayed on the page. Select the asset account to which we want to transfer (the transfer of assets into and out of the asset account will remain the same).

  7. Click Select Transfer In Number.

  8. Choose to search by Telephone Number or Account Id

  9. The background calls the three-family information interface according to the mobile phone number or account id to get the three-family information (without querying balance information)

  10. Select Transfer method, currently supports percentage and fixed value transfer

  11. Enter the percentage or fixed amount, fill in the reason for the transfer, and submit the transfer

  12. The back office needs to determine the bill_type of the incoming and outgoing numbers. The two bill_types must be the same, and both post-paid or pre-paid must be paid at the same time, otherwise the error is thrown.

  13.
