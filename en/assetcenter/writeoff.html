# step1 Pull S file

 Pull the s file generated by billing to the account management host

# step2 S file audit

The statistical data of the total.log file given by billing is compared with that of the S file. If the amount and the number of records match, the verification is successful.

# Business restrictions:

  1. ~~ Cancel order ~~

    Ledgers are moving and cannot be cancelled

  2. Payment

    The account will be entered in the am_balance table of the previous month and the current month, only the account will be entered, no account will be written off, and no work order will be inserted

  3. Transfer

  4. Reconciliation

    Reconciliation of bills generated during the month is not allowed

  5. ~~ Pre-stored and cleared ~~

    账本在搬迁,不能清退

# step5 Modify the status of the account period

The bill and arrears files are stored in the warehouse and am_cycle table status is changed to 3

# step6 During customer inspection

The customer checked the bill for errors, and the check period was changed to 2
The status is successfully modified, and the zero payment work order is started at the beginning of the month.

# Business restrictions:

  1. Pay a fee

    Pay synchronously to determine whether there are unprocessed work orders, if there are work orders, insert the work orders, otherwise write off directly

  2. Cancel order

    3 status, there is no work order cancellation

  3. Transfer

    Can transfer, but there are work orders that do not allow transfer

  4. ~~ reconciliation ~~ ???

  5. Pre-stored and retired

    Work orders are not allowed to clear

# step5 Processing of 0 payment work order completed at the beginning of month

 Change the accounting period status to 4

# Business restrictions:

20 status: check real-time bills for two months

Status change time: Automatically cut at the beginning of the month 20

not support:

Ledger, transfer, cancellation, clearing, cancellation

stand by:

Pay

3 Status: Check real-time bills for one month only

Status change time: Completed cut of bill

not support:

Reconcile monthly bills and write off

Semi-supported

Transfer (there is a cancellation work order, no transfer is allowed)

stand by:

Payment of fees, transfer to monthly bills, transfer of books, cancellation of bills, clearing (whether or not clearing will be done after the 5th)

In the above two states, 4106 work orders are generated, 20 books are entered for two months, and 3 accounts are entered for one month.

Current month's inventory statement cannot be adjusted

2 status: Payment can be written off, but it is necessary to determine whether there is a zero payment work order of 4106. There is no direct write-off, and there is no write-off.

Status change time: The customer check is completed, and it will be switched off when the 4106 cancellation is started.

Current status <= 2

not support:

Semi-supported

Transfer (existing work order, no transfer allowed)

stand by:

Payment, bill adjustment, account book adjustment, cancellation, clearing (whether the clearing will be done after 5th), write-off
